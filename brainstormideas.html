Brainstorm Ideas

I took several HCI (Human-Computer Interaction) courses during my master’s program, so I have some knowledge in this area.

When you put on the VR headset and enter the virtual world, I envision this product as an app within that space.

We would need to collaborate with companies like Apple or Facebook to build an immersive world full of imagination.

Make our product to be an app in the universe. People could experience a narrative by walking into it, interacting with characters they’ve created.

I see great potential in this idea.
